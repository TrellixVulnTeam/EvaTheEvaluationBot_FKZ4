How to use Hubert

You can talk to me by typing in your message into the textfield or click one of my suggestions. If for some reason you don’t have an answer to a question you can skip the question at any time, by clicking on the skip button next to the textfield or ask me to skip it.

If you like to talk to me besides the evaluation you can ask me some questions. I’m able to switch to a “Fun-Mode” where we can do small talk. For example, I can try to improve your mood with some jokes 🤡

Type in: “Can you tell me a joke?”, or something similar.

I’m not all-knowing and might have to refer you to my big brother Google for complicated questions. But I will try my best!

I would also love to receive some feedback from you.

You can either give me feedback by clicking the “Feedback” button at any time or you will have the chance at the end of the Evaluation.

If you would like to receive the evaluation results, you can provide your e-mail address. Your data will be stored anonymously and not related to your evaluation results or to your person.

At the end of the evaluation you are able to see your given answers.
